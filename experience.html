FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What attractions are close to Keisha Luxury Furnished Apartment Bukoto?
We are located approximately a 15-minute ride from the Ndere Cultural Centre, 3.4 km from the Uganda National Cultural Heritage Museum, and just 1.9 km from Kabalagala.
Does the apartment provide parking?
Yes! We offer free parking on-site for all our guests.
What is the check-in and check-out time at the accommodation?
Check-in begins at 12:00 and continues until 23:30. Check-out is scheduled between 10:00 and 11:00 am.
Does the apartment have a kitchen?
Yes, guests will be able to make use of a fully-fitted kitchen equipped with a microwave, an electric kettle, a fridge/freezer, and a stove.
